Interaction Flow

John comes home from a long weekend at work and wants to watch one of the documentaries that he has been sucked into the last week. He opens his account and finds that his wife has been watching some drama series and is getting suggested more and his recently played is full of all the currently watching shows she has been starting and never finishing. He decides to create her own profile, so she can keep it separate from his, and he can easily resume his documentaries.

Precondition:

Johnny is logged onto his own Netflix account

Postcondition:

His wife will have her own Netflix account

Flow Steps:

  • John logs onto his computer and goes to Netflix in the browser
  • Since he has his own account he can create another user profile
  • Goes to the list of users and selects create profile
  • The site begins a set of prompts
  • Names profile after his wife and selects an avatar for her
  • Is prompted to select the primary language for shows and movies
  • Picks a few of her favorite shows to set the algorithm
  • The site creates a new profile under John's account
